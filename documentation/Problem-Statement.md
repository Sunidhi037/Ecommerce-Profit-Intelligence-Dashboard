# Problem Statement

## Background

The rapid growth of the e-commerce industry has enabled businesses to generate higher sales through promotional campaigns, product diversification, and aggressive discounting. However, increasing revenue does not always result in higher profitability. Factors such as excessive discounts, inefficient shipping strategies, low-margin products, and uneven regional performance often lead to significant profit leakage.

To remain competitive, organizations require data-driven insights that help management understand the reasons behind declining profit margins and identify opportunities for sustainable growth.

---

## Business Problem

An e-commerce company has experienced steady growth in sales over the past few years. Despite this positive trend, overall profit has remained relatively stagnant. Management suspects that high discount rates, operational inefficiencies, and product-level performance issues are reducing profitability but lacks sufficient analytical visibility to confirm these assumptions.

Additionally, the company has limited insight into customer purchasing behavior, regional performance, and shipping efficiency, making strategic decision-making increasingly difficult.

The organization requires an interactive analytical solution that consolidates key business metrics, identifies areas of profit leakage, and supports evidence-based decision-making.

---

## Objective

The objective of this project is to design an interactive Microsoft Excel dashboard that transforms raw transactional data into meaningful business intelligence.

The dashboard will help stakeholders monitor overall business performance, identify the primary causes of profit leakage, evaluate customer and product performance, and support strategic decision-making through clear, data-driven insights.
