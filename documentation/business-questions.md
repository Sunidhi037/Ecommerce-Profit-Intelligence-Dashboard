# Business Questions

The dashboard is designed to answer the following business questions.

## Executive Performance

- Is the business growing in terms of sales and profitability?

- How do current business KPIs compare across different periods?

- Which performance indicators require immediate management attention?

---

## Profitability Analysis

- Which product categories generate the highest and lowest profit?

- Which individual products consistently contribute to losses?

- Which regions and states are the least profitable?

- How does profit vary across different customer segments?

---

## Discount Analysis

- How do discounts impact profitability?

- Which discount ranges generate the highest revenue without significantly reducing profit?

- Are there products or categories where excessive discounting leads to losses?

---

## Customer Analysis

- Who are the highest-value customers based on revenue and profit?

- Which customer segment contributes the most to business growth?

- Which customers should be prioritized for retention initiatives?

---

## Sales Performance

- How have sales changed over time?

- Which months demonstrate peak and low business performance?

- Which product categories contribute the highest share of total revenue?

---

## Shipping Performance

- Which shipping mode is used most frequently?

- What is the average shipping time?

- Does shipping performance have any relationship with profitability?

---

## Decision Support

At the end of the analysis, the dashboard should provide sufficient evidence to answer one overarching business question:

> **What strategic actions should management take to improve profitability while maintaining sustainable sales growth?**
